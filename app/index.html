Vision & Mission

Vision 異象

In the footsteps of the Lord Jesus, we are committed to reach out and to serve cancer patients and their family members with Christ’s love.

跟隨主耶穌的腳踪和榜樣，以基督之愛接觸及服事癌病患者及其家屬。

Mission 使命

To reach out to cancer patients and their family members with Christ’s love to provide them with wholistic care in areas of physical, emotional, spiritual and social needs.

以基督的愛接觸及服事癌病患者及其家屬，包括身、心、靈及社群方面，提供全人關懷。

Statement of Faith on "Death & Euthanasia"

We believe that life is given by God, and that human beings are made in God's image, so we should respect them.

Human beings are mortal, and inevitably will have sickness and die.

People who are old or sick, and who are near the end of earthly life should be respected and valued as any other human being.

No human being has the authority to take the life of any innocent person, even if that person wants to terminate it.

We work with the health care professionals to provide the best possible emotional, spiritual and social support. We recognise and respect the importance of not actively hastening the process of death of human life.

The process of dying is spiritually important, and many people may reconcile with the Saviour if suitable opportunity arises. We provide holistic care for people who are dying, and for those who are close to them.

有關" 死亡及安樂死" 之信仰聲明

我們相信生命是神所賜與的，神以祂的形象造人，所以我們要尊重人。

人人必有一死，疾病及死亡是無法避免的。

生病老弱的、以及接近終結在世生命的人，應像其他人一樣，獲得尊重及重視。

沒有人享有權力奪去任何無辜者的生命，儘管這人想終結自己的生命。

我們跟護理專業人員合作，提供最佳可能的心、靈及社群的關懷。我們同時承認並尊重非主動地加速死亡過程之重要性。

死亡過程在靈性上是重要的。如果有合適的時機出現，許多人會與救主耶穌修和。我們會為垂死者及親人提供全人關懷。
